About Me

I am...Iron Man. Just kidding! My name is Ubonwan, which means Golden Lotus. It is pronouced the same way it is spelled: Oo-bon-wan. Despite what many people believe, I was actually not named after Obi-Wan Kenobi. Though, it would be cool if I was a legendary Jedi Master.

I am originally from Thailand; born in Bangkok and raised in Phichit, which is a small province positioned in the southern part of the northern region.

I recently graduated with a Bachelor in Hospitality Management and have previously received an opportunity to work at Epcot and Disney's Beach Club Resort. The most exciting thing that happened to me there was that I met Hugh Jackman aka Wolverine. Up until today, I still can't shut up about that (he touched my shoulder!)
